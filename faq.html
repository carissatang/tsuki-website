FAQs

Some common questions we receive, answered for you. If you have more questions, feel free to contact us or just drop by and say hello!




üì¶ On orders and shipping


Do you offer shipping?

    Yes, the shipping fee is $2.00 for all orders within Canada. We also offer arrangements for pick up at designated locations, for no charge. To finalize order logistics, we will email you and send you an Instagram DM.


Can I refund or exchange my item?

   No, all items are on final sale - we do not offer returns or exchange. However, under extenuating circumstances, we may make exceptions.


What if I want to change my order?

   You can change your order up until we process the payment.



üí≥ On financial transparency


How much and when do you donate to local charities?

    We donate 100% of profits to local charities every quarter (3 months). We also release all donation receipts on our social media platforms.


Which local charities do you support and how?

   We donate to local charities supporting women and children with care packages and monetary donations. Check out our social media platforms for the names of the charities we support!
